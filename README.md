## 📑 Featured Research

### 📄 Dual Anchors: China-U.S. Factors Jointly Determining Global Gold and Crude Oil Pricing
*— Systematic Evidence Based on Exhaustive Testing with VIF Constraints*

- **🥇 Gold Model (R²=0.908):** TIPS Real Yield + China-U.S. Yield Spread + Gold-Oil Ratio  
- **🛢️ Crude Oil Model (R²=0.889):** Shanghai Crude Oil Futures (SC) + VIX + Synthetic Time Spread + China-U.S. Yield Spread  

**The China-U.S. yield spread is the only common factor entering both optimal models.**  
Its pricing function in gold traces back to at least 2006; in crude oil, it was strictly activated by the launch of Shanghai crude oil futures in 2018.
