I study global commodity pricing, macro-finance, and the quantifiable role of China in international asset markets. My current research focuses on the China-U.S. dual-anchor structure in gold and crude oil pricing.
📄 Dual Anchors: China-U.S. Factors Jointly Determining Global Gold and Crude Oil Pricing
Systematic Evidence Based on Exhaustive Testing with VIF Constraints • SSRN Working Paper, May 2026 • SSRN ID 6740898
Using exhaustive testing (462 gold / 141 crude oil factor combinations), rolling regressions and VIF constraints, this study demonstrates that the China-U.S. yield spread is the only common factor entering both optimal gold and crude oil pricing models.
