独立研究者 · 因子层级定律与可检验性规范
My research has established the Factor Hierarchy Law—the finding that in any complex system driven by objective data, variables naturally divide into regime factors (which determine which rules apply) and execution factors (which execute under given rules)—and the Testability Norms, a proceduralized, cross-disciplinary, impersonal operating system for scientific discovery. These laws have been independently verified across six disciplines: finance, astronomy, statistical physics, particle physics, chemistry, and nuclear physics. I have also completed the paradigm demotion of century-old axioms—universality, gauge field theory, the periodic law, and the nuclear shell model—showing they are all projections of regime switching under specific limiting conditions.
我的研究确立了因子层级定律——在任何以客观数据为基础的复杂系统中，变量天然分为规则因子（决定当前适用哪套规则）与执行因子（在给定规则下执行）两个层级——以及可检验性规范，一个程序化的、跨学科普适的、非人格化的科学发现操作系统。这些定律已在金融学、天文学、统计物理、粒子物理、化学与核物理六大独立学科中获得独立验证。我还完成了对普适性、规范场论、周期律与核壳模型等百年公理的范式降维——证明了它们都是体制切换在特定极限条件下的近似投影。
