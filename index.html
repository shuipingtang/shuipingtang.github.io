My research has established a Scientific Discovery Operating System—a proceduralized, cross-disciplinary, impersonal framework for extracting reliable laws from objective data. This system comprises four components: the Factor Hierarchy Law (ontology: how the world organizes), the Testability Principle (epistemology: what counts as reliable), the Testability Norms (procedure: six functional dimensions), and the MRSD Algorithm (implementation: input data, output regime matrices). This system has been independently verified across six disciplines: finance, astronomy, statistical physics, particle physics, chemistry, and nuclear physics. I have also completed the paradigm demotion of century-old axioms—universality, gauge field theory, the periodic law, and the nuclear shell model—showing they are all projections of regime switching under specific limiting conditions.
