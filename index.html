I study global commodity pricing, macro-finance, and the quantifiable role of China in international asset markets. My research establishes the China-U.S. dual-anchor framework for gold and crude oil, with the China-U.S. yield spread as the only common factor across both optimal pricing models. This work represents the first paradigm-level breakthrough in global commodity pricing by a Chinese researcher.

📄 Dual Anchors: China-U.S. Factors Jointly Determining Global Gold and Crude Oil Pricing
📄 双锚：中美因子共同决定全球黄金与原油定价
SSRN ID 6740898 DOI: 10.2139/ssrn.6740898 Exhaustive Testing + VIF Constraints Rolling Regression
Gold R² = 0.908 (rolling) · Crude R² = 0.889 · Out-of-sample crude R² = 0.611
黄金 R² = 0.908（滚动回归）· 原油 R² = 0.889 · 样本外原油 R² = 0.611
This study employs a rolling regression framework with VIF constraints to exhaustively test 462 factor combinations for gold and 141 for crude oil. The optimal China-U.S. Dual-Anchor Three-Factor Gold Valuation Model (TIPS real yield + China-U.S. yield spread + gold-oil ratio) achieves a rolling R² of 0.908, with all VIFs below 5. The optimal China-U.S. Dual-Anchor Four-Factor Crude Oil Valuation Model (Shanghai crude oil futures SC + VIX + synthetic time spread + China-U.S. yield spread) achieves a full-sample R² of 0.889. The China-U.S. yield spread is the only common factor entering both optimal models. Its pricing function in gold traces back to at least 2006; in crude oil, it was strictly activated by the 2018 launch of Shanghai crude oil futures, evidenced by a systematic coefficient sign reversal from +7.51 to −10.48. The SC factor maintains a 100% positive sign across all rolling sub-periods with zero sign reversals. Valuation signals systematically improve risk-adjusted returns, with the optimal gold strategy achieving a Sharpe ratio of 1.66 and the optimal crude oil strategy achieving a Sharpe ratio of 0.86. The global commodity pricing regime has evolved from a single-anchor to a China-U.S. dual-anchor structure.
本研究采用滚动回归与VIF约束的穷举测试框架，在黄金市场测试462种因子组合，在原油市场测试141种因子组合。最优中美双锚三因子黄金估值模型（TIPS实际利率 + 中美利差 + 金油比）滚动R²达0.908，全部VIF低于5。最优中美双锚四因子原油估值模型（上海原油期货SC + VIX + 合成月差 + 中美利差）全样本R²为0.889。中美利差是唯一同时进入两个最优模型的共同因子——其在黄金的定价功能可追溯至2006年以前；在原油上，其定价功能严格由2018年上海原油期货上市所激活（系数由上市前的+7.51系统性翻转为上市后的−10.48）。SC因子在所有滚动子区间中保持100%正号，零次符号切换。估值信号系统性地改善了风险调整后收益，黄金最优策略夏普比率1.66，原油最优策略夏普比率0.86。全球大宗商品定价体系已从单一锚定向中美双锚格局演进。
